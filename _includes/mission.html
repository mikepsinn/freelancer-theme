My Mission

My primary interest at the moment is in accelerating clinical research through crowd-sourcing so that we may end the suffering caused by chronic illness.

Before my untimely death, I would like to optimize societal resource allocation in a manner than minimizes suffering. So I am devoting my time to efforts that can produce the most value for others.

However, my ultimate goal is a world that uses data to drive public policy which maximizes gross national health and happiness.

Please contact me if you're interested in working to minimize suffering in the universe.
